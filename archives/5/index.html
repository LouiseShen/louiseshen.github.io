Add Google’s reCAPTCHA to a html page is quite easy. In this article we are going to explain how to integrate Google’s reCAPTCHA in typecho.

Introduction

In typecho, admin/login.php is the login form while var/Widget/Login.php is our back end to accept the reCAPTCHA validation.

Register your website

Registering your website to use reCAPTCHA is pretty straight forward. Just visit https://google.com/recaptcha/admin to get a key & key secret.

If Google is unreachable in Your country or region like in China, you certainly do need a proxy. And only if your web server cannot access Google, it is not a big deal. We use recaptcha.net instead of google.com which the former is a official reverse proxy for Google’s reCAPTCHA.

Also in here, I choose the V2 checkbox.

Once you register your site, we just have to integrate it into typecho.

Add Google’s reCAPTCHA in the form

This step is to inclue the Google’s reCAPTCHA to typecho.
